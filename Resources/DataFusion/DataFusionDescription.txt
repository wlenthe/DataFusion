<h3>DataFusion Plugin</h3>

<p>This plugin implements filters required to merge 2 datasets of the same sample onto a single shared sampling grid, match the feature ids of their segmentations, and align their orientation reference frames using the algorithms described in [1].</p>

<h4>Citations</h4>
<p>
[1] Lenthe, W. C., Echlin, M. P., Trenkle, A., Syha, M., Gumbsch, P., & Pollock, T. M. (2015). Quantitative voxel-to-voxel comparison of TriBeam and DCT strontium titanate three-dimensional data sets. Journal of Applied Crystallography, 48(4), 1034-1046. 
<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1107/S1600576715009231">doi:10.1107/S1600576715009231</a>
